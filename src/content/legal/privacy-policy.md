**Email:** info@alphatrack.digital
**Website:** https://alphatrack.digital/

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### Contact and Enquiry Information

This may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, job title, website URL, country, and any information you provide when you complete a form, request a consultation, subscribe, or communicate with us.

### Website, Device, and Usage Information

This may include pages visited, links clicked, time spent on pages, referral sources, campaign source information, IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, screen size, language settings, and approximate location derived from IP address or analytics tools. We do not intentionally collect precise GPS location from website visitors.

### Marketing and Communication Information

This may include newsletter subscriptions, communication preferences, email engagement, campaign interactions, and responses to our marketing communications.

We collect information:

- Directly from you when you submit forms, send emails, call us, request proposals, or communicate with us.

- From public sources or professional platforms where relevant to enquiries, partnerships, or service delivery.

We use personal information to:

- Respond to enquiries, consultation requests, and business communications.
- Provide, manage, and improve our services.
- Send newsletters, marketing emails, and service updates where permitted by law or based on your consent.
- Analyze website traffic, user behaviour, campaign performance, and advertising effectiveness.
- Improve our website, content, services, reporting, and user experience.
- Deliver relevant marketing, retargeting, or advertising where permitted.

## 4. Lawful Basis for Processing

Where required by applicable data protection laws, we rely on one or more lawful bases, including:

- **Consent:** where you have agreed to receive marketing communications or accepted certain cookies and tracking technologies.
- **Contractual necessity:** where processing is needed to provide requested services or take steps before entering into a business relationship.
- **Legitimate interests:** where processing is necessary for business operations, service improvement, analytics, marketing, security, and client relationship management, provided your rights are not overridden.
- **Legal obligation:** where processing is required by applicable laws, regulations, or lawful requests.

We use cookies and similar technologies such as pixels, tags, beacons, and analytics scripts for website functionality, analytics, performance measurement, advertising, and marketing purposes.

These technologies help us keep the website functional and secure, understand how visitors use it, measure traffic and campaign performance, improve content, and deliver relevant advertising or retargeting where permitted.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings and, where available, through our cookie preference center. Our Cookie Policy is available at:

**https://alphatrack.digital/cookie-policy/**
